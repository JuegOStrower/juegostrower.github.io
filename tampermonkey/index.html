How to add Tampermokey to your computer

Adding Tampermokey to your computer is very easy. It is a simple extension for your browser. With Tampermonkey you can run my Smart Addons, my userscripts and the userscipt from other scratchers. This is an addon for scratch. You can add it by clicking this universal link or picking manually the link for your browser in the tampermonkey website.

What happened to all the projects about Smart Addons and userscript?

The answer is very simple, Scratch doesn't allow them anymore. Althought, you can continue using them. The Scratch Team only has blocked anouncing userscript in the website. I had to move all my smart addons (userscripts) to smart bookmarks (javascript clickeable bookmarks). You can see the original discuss in this link.
