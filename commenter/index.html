What's a CSRF token?

To check that you are not a robot, Scratch needs a 32 digs number to post comments. So, to get it you need to go to Scratch and click the "Secure" text in chrome (up & left). Then click "Cookies", expand "scratch.mit.edu", expand "cookies" and then click "scratchcsrftoken". Finally, write in this page the number provided in "Content".
